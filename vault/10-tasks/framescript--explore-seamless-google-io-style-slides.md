# Explore seamless Google I/O-style slides in FrameScript

## Outcome

Decide whether FrameScript is a plausible tool for high-continuity presentation slides, and identify the smallest prototype that would prove it.

## Notes

The point is not to copy a specific event style, but to test the technique: one continuous scene, camera/state transitions, staged reveal timing, and exportability for a live talk.
